About Us

Bappa Youth is an Ganesh association maintained by some youngster's in Beedi Workers Colony, Kamareddy, Telangana State.

We Started our first Ganesh on 2020 with a small budget, we celebrate ganesh festival with our friends and family. All the colony members supported our Youth

Since 2023 we are supposed to put Durga Matha but in 2024 we also put Durga Matha in our colony.

Address:

Beedi Workers Colony, Kamareddy, Telangana State.
